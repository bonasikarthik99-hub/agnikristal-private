AgniKristal

AI-Assisted Pharmaceutical Co-Crystal Screening Platform

About

AgniKristal is a computational platform developed for screening API–coformer combinations in pharmaceutical crystal engineering. It integrates ΔpKa prediction, hydrogen-bond interaction analysis, synthon scoring, and 3D molecular visualization.

Key Features

ΔpKa Prediction

Predict proton transfer likelihood between API and coformer.

Synthon Analysis

Identify hydrogen-bonding synthons in crystal structures.

3D Visualization

Interactive molecular visualization of API-coformer pairs.

Automated Reports

Generate PDF screening reports with scoring results.
